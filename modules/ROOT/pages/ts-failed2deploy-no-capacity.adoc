== Cause

You do not have sufficient vCore capacity to deploy the applications in order to support the message flow.

== Solutions

* Contact your MuleSoft Platform Administrator to review the vCore allocation for your business group.

* Allow 15 minutes after the vCores are added to your business group before redeploying.
