= Configure Host
ifndef::env-site,env-github[]
include::_attributes.adoc[]
endif::[]

Your organization is considered the host partner in Anypoint Partner Manager.

Within host page you can:

** Add B2B identifiers
** Add certificates your organization owns
** Configure global validation and acknowledgement settings, and
** Configure the storage location for transmission payloads.

== To configure the host:
. From within your environment, select name of your organization from the left-hand navigation. +
. Click the *New Partner* button and fill in the receiving endpoint. +

From here you can check the Reference section for detailed information on various settings.
